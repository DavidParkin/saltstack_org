Communicate

Salt benefits from a vibrant and growing community. Our goal is to build a community where we learn from each other and where we can reach our goals together.

Joining the community is easy, join the Salt development on GitHub, Chat with the team in IRC, and get updates and detailed information on the mailing list.

Our community is continuously developing Salt screencasts, watch screencasts demonstrating Salt’s capabilities and tricks users have implemented using Salt’s tools. Discover how you can contribute screencasts and presentations.

IRC

Chat and ask questions in the #salt channel on Freenode. Get to know the community!

New to IRC? IRC Tips

  • Search the mailing list first.
  • Search the IRC logs first.
  • No introductions needed.
    There's no need to introduce yourself or ask "Can I ask a question?" Just ask!
  • Stay logged in and wait for an answer.
    If you don't get an answer immediately, don't give up. The person with the answer might be busy coding or afk. Leave your window open and someone will reply to you.
  • Use Freenode's webchat interface to connect without installing any software.
